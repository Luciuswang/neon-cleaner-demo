# A0-S01 Approved Motion Plan

## Approved Still

Approved still frame:

```text
source/storyboard/approved/SB-A0-01_establishing_world.png
```

This frame is now the approved direction for the opening world-establishing shot.

## Shot Purpose

This shot must do four things immediately:

1. prove this is `post-war future San Francisco`
2. show the `layered elevated chase route`
3. signal that a larger war is still happening beyond the street level
4. pull the audience into the road system that will soon matter to Lin Xia

## Recommended Duration

```text
6 to 7 seconds
```

Do not make this shot too long. It should feel grand, but still be a clean setup beat.

## Camera Move

Recommended move:

```text
half-bird's-eye slow descending drift
```

Detailed motion:

1. Start with a very brief hold, around `8 to 12 frames`
   Let the audience register the skyline, storm, bridge, and elevated road system.

2. Begin a slow forward drift plus slight downward descent
   The camera should feel heavy and expensive, like a helicopter or stabilized aerial plate, not like a drone vlog.

3. Add a subtle lateral bias toward the main elevated route
   The motion should gently favor the broken freeway corridor that will become the chase path.

4. End with the elevated route and its broken ramp more visually dominant than the skyline
   By the final second, the audience should already feel where the story is about to move.

## What Should Move Inside the Frame

Keep motion layered and restrained:

- thunderclouds roll slowly
- distant lightning flashes in the storm bank
- smoke columns curl and drift
- tiny distant battle glints or tracer streaks flicker in the far skyline
- rain haze shifts across the bridge and downtown
- practical emergency lights pulse softly on lower roads
- small reflections shimmer on wet asphalt

Do not add:

- giant foreground explosions
- fast camera shake
- screen-filling VFX noise
- too many obvious aircraft crossing frame

The world should feel alive and under stress, but still readable.

## Tone

The emotional read is:

```text
the world is losing, but not lost
```

This shot is not just destruction. It must carry:

- dread
- scale
- weather pressure
- one faint thread of human resistance

## Best Transition To Shot 2

Recommended next shot:

```text
A0-S02 Lin Xia reveal from behind / three-quarter rear on the overpass
```

Best transition logic:

1. Shot 1 ends by favoring the elevated route.
2. Cut to Lin Xia already standing above that same route.
3. Keep the city geography consistent between the two shots.

This creates a clean story read:

```text
world -> route -> watcher of the route
```

That is stronger than cutting randomly to a face close-up.

## Suggested Cut Style

Best option:

```text
hard cut on a lightning flash or distant battle flare
```

Why it works:

- gives energy without feeling like a music video
- hides the change in scale from wide world shot to heroine medium-wide shot
- keeps the war atmosphere active

Alternative:

```text
cut on motion settle
```

As the camera finishes its subtle descent toward the elevated corridor, cut to Lin Xia framed above the same corridor from a closer angle.

## Recommended Shot 2 Composition

For `A0-S02`, avoid a beauty close-up first.

Start with:

- rear or three-quarter rear silhouette
- Lin Xia small but readable in frame
- coat moving in cold wind
- city and elevated route still visible below

Then, if needed, later push closer in `A0-S02` or `A0-S03`.

## Why This Order Works

This structure gives you:

1. `world scale`
2. `hero presence`
3. `target reveal`
4. `launch into chase`

That is more cinematic and easier to cut than starting from face or cockpit too early.

## Tool Recommendation

For this exact opening shot, use:

```text
approved still -> image-to-video
```

Do not start from raw text-to-video if you already have this still approved.

The still already solved:

- world layout
- bridge placement
- road hierarchy
- storm tone
- battle scale

The video model should only solve:

- subtle camera descent
- sky movement
- smoke drift
- lightning
- distant battle flicker
