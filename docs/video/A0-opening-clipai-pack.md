# A0 Opening ClipAI Pack

这个包给 `ClipAI` 直接使用。

目标：

- 让 `A0` 开场四个镜头能连续生成
- 先保证 `世界观 -> 林夏 -> 目标 -> 进入追击` 的电影语法成立
- 每条镜头都能单独重生，再从结果里选 through 版

参考素材：

```text
世界参考：
source/storyboard/approved/SB-A0-01_establishing_world.png

角色参考：
web/assets/女主.png
```

## 使用原则

1. `A0-S01`
   优先 `I2V`

2. `A0-S02`
   可以 `I2V` 或 `T2V + 角色参考图`

3. `A0-S03`
   可以先 `T2V`

4. `A0-S04`
   如果前面通过，再做

5. 每条都先试：

```text
5 秒
6 秒
```

然后再从较稳那版继续收。

## A0-S01 世界建立镜头

### 镜头目标

先把世界、战争、旧金山高差高架系统讲清楚，再把观众视线收向之后要追逐的那条高架路线。

### 正向描述

```text
Use the uploaded still as a strict composition and geography reference. Keep the exact skyline layout, Golden Gate Bridge silhouette, elevated freeway stacking, broken ramp shape, street alignment, city height difference, and horizon line stable.

This is a premium cinematic opening establishing shot of post-war future San Francisco in 2037, just after heavy rain, with another storm front pressing in. The world has been damaged by an ongoing war, and far in the distance the conflict between carbon-based human forces and silicon-based machine forces is still active. The subject of the shot is the wounded city itself, not a character.

The image must feel like a big-budget live-action science-fiction war film opening plate, not concept art, not a VFX demo, not a game cinematic. Use a cold blue-gray storm palette with restrained magenta emergency practicals. The sky is dense with layered storm clouds, heavy thunderheads, distant lightning inside the cloud bank, drifting smoke columns, rain haze, and faint battlefield residue. Wet freeway concrete and soaked asphalt should show realistic reflections, grounded material response, volumetric haze, atmospheric depth, subtle lens halation, controlled bloom, and premium anamorphic film rendering.

Duration is 6 seconds. Begin with a brief hold, then perform a very slow forward push with a slight downward descent. The camera must feel heavy, stable, and expensive, like a stabilized helicopter or high-end aerial plate, never like a drone vlog. Internal motion comes mainly from slowly rolling storm clouds, distant lightning pulses, drifting smoke, subtle shimmer in wet reflections, and tiny but readable far-background combat flashes and tracer streaks between carbon-based and silicon-based forces. Keep those battle signs distant and small in scale.

By the final second, make the broken elevated chase corridor slightly more visually dominant than the skyline, so the next shot can cut naturally to Lin Xia standing above the same route. The emotional read is: the world is close to losing, but hope is not gone.
```

### 运镜描述

```text
Very slow cinematic push forward and slightly downward. No rotation, no sudden zoom, no handheld shake, no aggressive parallax. Architecture remains rigid and stable. Motion comes from clouds, smoke, haze, lightning pulses, wet-surface shimmer, and tiny far-distance battle glints. Heavy, restrained, expensive, live-action feeling.
```

### 负向限制

```text
warped architecture, distorted bridge, melting freeway, bent skyline, unstable buildings, drone vlog movement, handheld shake, sudden zoom, whip pan, fisheye distortion, close aircraft flyby, large foreground explosion, VFX demo look, cheap CG, generic concept art, low-detail storm clouds, empty sky, toy city, game screenshot, HUD, subtitles, watermark, logo, anime, cartoon, overprocessed HDR, plastic materials
```

### 结尾切点

```text
lightning flash or distant battle flare
```

## A0-S02 林夏出场

### 镜头目标

不要一上来就是美颜特写。先让林夏以“站在同一路线之上的人类希望”出现，和上一镜的世界与路线保持空间连续。

### 正向描述

```text
Use the uploaded heroine reference image as a strict identity anchor for Lin Xia's face, hair, coat, silhouette, age, and overall human feeling. She is an East Asian woman in her early 20s, slim athletic build, pale skin, black ponytail with loose front strands, sharp tired eyes, long black tactical leather coat with magenta inner lining and subtle magenta glowing strips near the hem, fitted black combat clothing, gloves, utility belt, and black boots.

This is the second opening shot of a premium live-action science-fiction war film. Lin Xia stands on a shattered overpass edge or rooftop overlooking the same post-war future San Francisco seen in the previous shot. The damaged elevated freeway corridor remains visible below her, with lower streets, broken infrastructure, smoke columns, distant lightning, and faint far-background battle signs between carbon-based human forces and silicon-based machine forces.

Do not stage her like a model or idol. She is burdened, exhausted, alert, and still holding on. Her body language must read as restrained determination, grief, pressure, and a thin thread of hope. Start from a rear or three-quarter rear composition so her silhouette and coat read first against the city. The wind moves her coat and loose hair strands. Keep the city geography readable behind and below her.

The image must feel expensive, grounded, and cinematic: realistic skin, leather, and wet fabric response, subtle lens halation, soft atmospheric diffusion, cold blue-gray storm palette with restrained magenta practicals, premium anamorphic rendering, and stable architecture. The final beat should motivate her eyeline down toward the elevated chase route.
```

### 运镜描述

```text
Begin on a rear or three-quarter rear medium-wide shot. Slow subtle lateral drift with a very gentle push-in, almost imperceptible. No fast movement. Let the motion come from wind in the coat, slight hair movement, drifting smoke in the background, and distant lightning pulses. End with her eyeline and body angle clearly favoring the chase corridor below.
```

### 负向限制

```text
fashion pose, idol pose, beauty close-up first, smiling expression, glossy ad lighting, mannequin face, cross-eyed, asymmetrical eyes, anime heroine, exposed midriff, floating hair without wind logic, warped city, bad anatomy, poster pose, plastic leather, game cutscene look, text, watermark, HUD, subtitles
```

### 结尾切点

```text
cut on eyeline motivation toward the route below
```

## A0-S03 敌方装甲车出现

### 镜头目标

把追逐目标明确化。不是普通车流，而是一个可以一眼锁定、能支撑后面追车的敌方装甲目标。

### 正向描述

```text
This is a premium live-action sci-fi action thriller target-reveal shot. An armored black hostile vehicle or small convoy moves through a partially damaged elevated freeway ramp in post-war future San Francisco after rain. The lower city falls away on one side, making the route feel dangerous and elevated. The road is wet, cracked, and partially broken, with damaged guardrails, road debris, smoke, drifting mist, and restrained magenta emergency reflections.

The vehicle must feel heavy, reinforced, militarized, and real. Dirty wet panels, realistic scale, damaged lights, practical reflections, and a readable silhouette are essential. This is not a toy vehicle and not a stylized sci-fi hovercar. Keep it grounded in physical suspension, tire grip, and road contact.

The shot should feel like someone has just spotted the target that the heroine will pursue. Use premium cinematic rendering, real metal and wet paint response, atmospheric perspective, subtle lens bloom, depth compression, and expensive practical night lighting. Preserve the San Francisco elevated route identity and the city's high-versus-low road structure in the background.
```

### 运镜描述

```text
Low hidden observer angle from behind damaged concrete or guardrail cover. Very subtle forward tracking or slight rise as the hostile vehicle commits to the route. No chaotic camera movement. Let mist, smoke, wheel spray, and practical light reflections provide the motion energy.
```

### 负向限制

```text
toy car, hovercar, floating vehicle, vehicle changing shape, bad wheel contact, fake sparks, flat smoke, generic freeway, no city height difference, oversized sci-fi guns, foreground explosion covering vehicle, plastic materials, cartoon, anime, game HUD, text, watermark, logo
```

### 结尾切点

```text
vehicle direction and lane commitment must clearly set up pursuit
```

## A0-S04 林夏进入追击车

### 镜头目标

把“我要追上去”从人物情绪转成动作启动点，成为进入追逐的点火镜头。

### 正向描述

```text
Use the uploaded heroine reference as a strict identity anchor for Lin Xia. This is the ignition-point shot before a combat chase begins. Lin Xia drops into a low damaged futuristic pursuit car on a rain-soaked shattered overpass in post-war future San Francisco. The elevated route ahead is visible through cracked supports, drifting smoke, and wet glare. The city falls away below, maintaining the sense of height and danger established by previous shots.

Inside the vehicle, restrained magenta dashboard light wakes up against cold storm-blue ambient light. Rain beads on the windshield, practical reflections crawl across wet glass and metal, and the cockpit feels tactical, used, and real rather than overdesigned. Lin Xia's movement is efficient and urgent, not flashy. She is locking into action.

The shot should feel like the exact second before the chase begins. Premium live-action realism, grounded sci-fi, readable cockpit geometry, realistic wet materials, subtle atmospheric depth, controlled bloom, no game UI, and no fantasy clutter.
```

### 运镜描述

```text
Start slightly outside or just over the door line, then settle into a tight cockpit-oriented framing as she enters and the car wakes up. Small practical motion only: body drop, dashboard activation, windshield rain shift, slight chassis lurch. End with forward intent clearly established.
```

### 负向限制

```text
parked-car portrait, overstyled cockpit, racing game HUD, huge neon dashboard, fashion entrance, floating steering wheel, toy interior, impossible camera teleport, anime lighting, text, watermark, subtitles, logo
```

### 结尾切点

```text
dashboard wake-up or vehicle lurch into pursuit alignment
```

## 推荐批量顺序

先这样跑：

1. `A0-S01` 出 2 版
2. `A0-S02` 出 3 版
3. `A0-S03` 出 3 版
4. `A0-S04` 出 2 版

优先筛选标准：

- `A0-S01`
  世界稳不稳，高架追逐路线清不清楚
- `A0-S02`
  林夏像不像“人类侧最后还在反击的人”
- `A0-S03`
  敌方目标是不是一眼可读
- `A0-S04`
  有没有真正“进入追击”的启动感
